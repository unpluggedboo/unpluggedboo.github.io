About

I recently graduated from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute with a B.S. in Biomedical Engineering and a minor in Healthcare Markets.

I’m passionate about translational and interdisciplinary research, particularly in developing medical and wearable devices that advance personalized medicine and leverage technology to prevent and treat disease. My interests focus on innovations that address chronic conditions and mental health — areas where long-term solutions can meaningfully improve quality of life. I’m drawn to projects that span the full device pipeline, from problem identification and design to testing and iteration.

Currently, I’m applying to PhD programs and pursuing scholarships to support my graduate studies, with the goal of continuing to develop technologies that help people live longer, healthier lives.
